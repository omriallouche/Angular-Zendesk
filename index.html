--- layout: default title: AngularJS directives and service for Zendesk, allowing you to show a single or multiple Zendesk help articles, search for articles, or submit a Zendesk ticket ---
  • Show a single Zendesk Article
  • Show a Zendesk Article in a Tooltip (actually a Bootstrap Popover)
  • Show an Accordion of Zendesk Articles for a given category, tag, author
  • A searchbox for Zendesk articles that match your search string
  • Submit a Zendesk Ticket
