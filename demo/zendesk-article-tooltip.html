Show a tooltip with a Zendesk article

Note that the tooltip used is actually a Twitter Bootstrap popover, and can include HTML tags, including links, images and more.

With Links

Code

This page contains the directive

<a href="#" ot-zendesk-tooltip="" article-id="211790065" popover-placement="right">Show Zendesk Tooltip with an article that includes links</a>

The directive injects the contents of the Zendesk article 211790065, available at https://otonomic.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/211790065

Result

Show Zendesk Tooltip with an article that includes links

With Images

Code

This page contains the directive

<a href="#" ot-zendesk-tooltip="" article-id="211790065" popover-placement="right">Show Zendesk Tooltip with an article that includes an image</a>

The directive injects the contents of the Zendesk article 210774709, available at https://otonomic.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/210774709

Result

Show Zendesk Tooltip with an article that includes an image
